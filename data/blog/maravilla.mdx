One of the great things about living in Puerto Morelos is the renowned Ruta de los Cenotes. This highway connects Puerto Morelos with a small town called Leona Vicario and is home to more than 60 cenotes. Two of my favorite to dive are called Maravilla (The Wonder) and Zapote (aka Hells Bells). At the end of December 2025, my dive buddy Linda Elliott visited me from Canada and we dove both these cenotes.

Linda took such great photos where it shows the magic of the light shining down from the entrance of the cenote Maravilla. Below are two of my favorites.
